Girls Inc: Non-Profit Redesign

The Team wants to help the Houston local Girls Inc. to redesign the existing website, so users will be able to have easier access to it and support local underage girls in need. In this case, the local underage girls will get more attention from the society and have successful sponsorship and partnerships. At the same time, the team hopes the redesign can help the girls earn opportunities to better Education, Gender Equality and any other services.

Industry: Non-Profit
Project: Website Redesign (Mobile)
Role: UX/UI Designers (Research, Interactions, Visuals)
Tools: Miro, Figma, Trello, Google Forms

COMPETITORS

Girls on the run

A non-profit organization that is aimed to help young girls to achieve success and live better.
Need volunteers and donation. Mentoring and educating programs are set by grades and ages
Clear design and navigation. Show some organization stories on the homepage. Easy to sign up with donation and volunteer

Women in localization

A non-profit organization that is aimed to create / spread job opportunities for women in localization field.
Need volunteers.
Clear navigation. The website has different events listed, easy for users to sign up, easy for users to look up their own regional contact

Dallas, TX

An non-profit organization that that is aimed to help women and girls make choices toward becoming independent, productive and financially stable.
Need volunteers and donation.
Clear navigation. Appealing and consistent design. Pop up with sign up form. Easy access to CTA button.

USER PERSONA

Diana Harris, 32

Occupation: Teacher
Location: Houston
Salary: $60k /yr
Goals & Needs

Wants to feel a connection to the overall cause of the program
Wants to know she is making a difference when helping out an organization
Transparency is important and organization
Pains & Frustrations

Doesn’t like sketchy looking websites
Gets frustrated when she can’t find simple information
Hates guessing what the cause or mission is on a website

USERFLOW

1. Mission and Information about the organization
2. Fill out the volunteer application
