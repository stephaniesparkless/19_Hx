About Girls Inc

The Team wants to help the Houston local Girls Inc. to redesign the existing website, so users will be able to have easier access to it and support local underage girls in need. In this case, the local underage girls will get more attention from the society and have successful sponsorship and partnerships. At the same time, the team hopes the redesign can help the girls earn opportunities to better Education, Gender Equality and any other services.
